Traditional Turkish Foods

Lahmacun

A flatbread, often called Turkish pizza, topped with minced meat, tomato paste, garlic, and spices.

Doner Kebab

A popular dish made from stacked layers of lamb or beef, served in crusty bread with salad and sauce.

Baklava

A sweet pastry with filo layers, ground nuts, and honey syrup. A perfect Turkish dessert.

Famous Turkish Landmarks

Topkapi Palace

Once the primary residence of the Ottoman sultans, a historic palace in Istanbul with stunning gardens and artifacts.

Blue Mosque

Known for its stunning blue tiles and six minarets, this mosque is one of the most famous landmarks in Istanbul.

Bosphorus River

A natural strait that connects the Black Sea to the Sea of Marmara, forming a boundary between Asia and Europe.
